News Fashion Event Details

Date: 09-07-2024

Timing: 00:00

Registration fees: 20/-

Duration: 45 minutes

Venue: ---

Description:Participants will demonstrate their creativity by crafting outfits using only newspaper. This event challenges participants to innovate with limited resources while promoting artistic expression.

Judging Criteria:Entries will be evaluated based on creativity, execution, fit on the model, and effective use of newspaper material.

Rules and Regulations:

• Teams of three members: 2 designers + 1 model

• Simple preparations can be pre-made for efficiency.

• Use newspaper exclusively for creating the outfits.

• Outfits may include ornaments made from newspaper.

• Respectful and cooperative behavior expected from all participants.
