About Women in Engineering (WIE)

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is a global organization dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls to pursue careers in engineering. WIE envisions a vibrant, inclusive community where women use their diverse talents to innovate for humanity's benefit. It provides networking opportunities, mentorship programs, educational outreach, and professional development workshops. By fostering an environment that encourages leadership and growth, WIE strives to ensure women engineers are recognized and valued for their contributions to the field.
