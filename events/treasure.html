Treasure Hunt

Treasure Hunt Details

Date: 15-07-2024

Timing: 02:00 PM

Registration fees: 10/- per head

Duration: 60 minutes

Venue: Room No. B13

Description:Teams will race against time to solve clues and find hidden treasures around the campus.

Judging Criteria:

• Teams are judged on how quickly they complete the hunt.

• The number of treasures each team finds during the hunt

Rules and Regulations:

• Each team must consist of 4 members.

• Teams must solve each clue to find the next treasure.

• Teams can ask us for a maximum of two hints, but each hint will add a 5-minute penalty to their total time.
