Calligraphy

Calligraphy Event Details

Date: 10-07-2024

Timing: 10:00 AM

Registration fees: 20/-

Duration: 60 minutes

Venue: Room No. B08

Description: Participants will be provided with a specific quote related to women empowerment. Each participant will use their calligraphy skills to creatively design and present the given quote. This event aims to celebrate the art of calligraphy while promoting the powerful messages of women empowerment.

Judging Criteria: Entries will be judged based on creativity, originality, and adherence to calligraphy principles.

Rules and Regulations:

• Individual participation.

• Participants must bring their own calligraphy supplies; no materials will be provided by the organisers.

• Participants must use the provided quote for their calligraphy. Any deviation from the given quote will result in disqualification.
