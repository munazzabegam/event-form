Cooking without fire

Cooking without fire Details

Date: 13-07-2024

Timing: 01:30 AM

Registration fees: 10/- per head

Duration: 60 minutes

Venue: Room No. B13

Description:Participants will prepare delicious dishes using only raw ingredients and no cooking appliances.

Judging Criteria:

• Flavor and seasoning of the dish.

• Visual appeal and arrangement of the dish.

• Quality and variety of textures in the dish.

Rules and Regulations:

• •Each team consist of 3 members.

• Each team must bring their own ingredients and utensils.

• Participants should prepare a Starter, main dish, desert and a drink

• All preparation must be done on-site during the event.Pre-cooked or pre-processed foods are not allowed.
