Fruit Carving

Fruit Carving Details

Date: 18-07-2024

Timing: 11:00 AM

Registration fees: 10/- per head

Duration: 60 minutes

Venue: Room No. B08

Description:Participants will create stunning designs by carving various fruits. It's a blend of creativity, precision, and imagination.

Judging Criteria:

• The level of detail and accuracy in the carving.

• Overall appearance and arrangement of the carved fruit.

Rules and Regulations:

• Each team must consist of 2 members.

• All participants must bring their own carving tools and fruits.

• Designs must be original and created during the event.Pre-made or pre-carved fruits are not allowed.
