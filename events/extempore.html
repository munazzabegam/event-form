Extempore Event Details

Date: 09-07-2024

Timing: 12:00 PM

Registration fees: 20/- per head

Duration: 60 minutes

Venue: Room No. B08

Description:Participants will be provided with a randomly selected topic. Each participant will then deliver an impromptu speech on the given topic. This event aims to foster awareness, appreciation, and dialogue while promoting public speaking skills.

Judging Criteria:Entries will be judged based on content relevance, coherence, delivery, and engagement with the audience.

Rules and Regulations:

• Individual participation.

• Each participant will 2 minutes to speak on the given topic.

• Topics will be provided by the organizers and must be adhered to. Any deviation from the given topic will result in disqualification.

• Respectful and supportive behavior is expected from all participants and audience members.
